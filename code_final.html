Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us

How is this different from hiring a full-time designer? expand_more

We provide senior-level UX expertise without the overhead of a full-time hire. You get strategic thinking, rapid execution, and actionable deliverables without long-term commitments or the 3-month ramp-up time.

What if we're not happy with the results? expand_more

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the initial audit findings, we'll refund your investment, no questions asked. Our goal is to provide genuine value, not just deliverables.

Do you sign NDAs? expand_more

Absolutely. We understand the sensitive nature of product development and are happy to sign NDAs before any engagement. Your intellectual property and product details are completely confidential.

Can you work with our existing design system? expand_more

Yes! We work within your existing design system, component library, and brand guidelines. If you don't have one, we can help establish foundational patterns as part of the engagement.

What tools do you use for deliverables? expand_more

We primarily use Figma for design work, Loom for video walkthroughs, and Notion or Google Docs for documentation. We're flexible and can adapt to your team's preferred tools if needed.
